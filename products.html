What we carry

Groceries, halal meat,
and the bakery case.

A working overview of what you’ll find on the shelves. Stock changes weekly , call ahead if there’s a specific item you’re after.

01 , Groceries

From the world
to the shelf.

Relationships and agreements with vendors around the world bring the world closer to you. Local vendors keep our bread fresh daily.

Ask us about our own Jummah Bazaar branded super‑long basmati rice, as well as our own saffron originating from the finest source in the world. Pantry staples, oils, dried fruits, nuts, jams, teas, sauces, condiments, and shelves of spices waiting to be sniffed.

02 , Halal Meat

Cut fresh,
cut to order.

The finest halal meat shipped from local vendors and prepared fresh by expert butchers.

We carry goat, lamb, chicken, and beef as well as pre‑packaged and pre‑seasoned meats ready for the grill or the pot. Have a special order, a holiday tray, or a particular cut in mind? Call the counter , we’ll set it aside.

03 , Bakery

Bread daily.

We bake our own goods too. Come try our various sweets, pastries, and warm items made right here in the back.
